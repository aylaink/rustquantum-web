Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it.

Cookies we use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Cloudflare (__cf_bm) Strictly necessary Bot detection and security 30 minutes
Google Analytics (_ga) Analytics (opt-in) Distinguishes unique users 2 years
Google Analytics (_ga_RKXTH8PTT5) Analytics (opt-in) Persists session state 2 years

Strictly necessary cookies

These are required for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. Cloudflare sets security cookies automatically to protect against malicious traffic.

Analytics cookies (opt-in)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics cookies are not set by default: on your first visit a banner asks for explicit consent, and tracking only begins after you click Accept. If you click Reject (or close the banner), no analytics cookies are stored and no data leaves your browser.

When enabled, analytics data is anonymous (IPs are anonymised at collection) and used solely to count page views, referrers, country, and device type. No personally identifiable information is collected.

To revoke a previously granted consent: clear the site data for www.rustquantum.com in your browser settings, then reload — the banner will reappear.

How to manage cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

We may update this policy when we add or change services. Updates will be posted on this page.
