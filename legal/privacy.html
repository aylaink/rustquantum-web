Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Ignacio López Rodríguez (data controller under the GDPR), as a personal learning project, from Spain under the domain rustquantum.com. For any privacy-related request, email info@rustquantum.com or use the contact form.

What data we collect

Contact form

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The subject and message you write

This data is sent via Cloudflare Workers to the Resend email API, which delivers it to our inbox. We do not store your data on any server or database beyond what is necessary to receive and respond to your message.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a cookieless and privacy-preserving analytics service. It does not set cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, and does not persist any identifier on your device. It collects only aggregated page-view counts, referrer, country, and device category. No personal data is processed, so no consent banner is shown.

Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for details on what cookies this site uses.

How we use your data

  • Contact form data: solely to respond to your inquiry
  • Aggregated analytics metrics: to understand site usage and improve content

International data transfers

Some of the processors above are based in or transfer data to the United States:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. processes traffic (including Web Analytics beacons) across its global network including the United States, under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.
  • Resend processes email delivery in the United States under standard contractual clauses.

Data retention

Contact form messages are retained for up to 24 months unless an active conversation requires longer, after which they are deleted. Cloudflare Web Analytics retains aggregated metrics (no personal data) for up to six months.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, use the contact form.
