Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Ignacio López Rodríguez (data controller under the GDPR), as a personal learning project, from Spain under the domain rustquantum.com. For any privacy-related request, email info@rustquantum.com or use the contact form.

What data we collect

Contact form

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The subject and message you write

This data is sent via Cloudflare Workers to the Resend email API, which delivers it to our inbox. We do not store your data on any server or database beyond what is necessary to receive and respond to your message.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (Measurement ID G-RKXTH8PTT5) to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics is opt-in: nothing is loaded or stored until you click Accept on the consent banner shown on first visit. If you accept, anonymous data is collected (pages visited, referrer, country, device type) with IP anonymisation enabled.

Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for details on what cookies this site uses.

How we use your data

  • Contact form data: solely to respond to your inquiry
  • Analytics data (only with your consent): to understand site usage and improve content

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, use the contact form.
